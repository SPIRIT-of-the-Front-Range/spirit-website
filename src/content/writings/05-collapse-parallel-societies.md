We are in an era of widespread institutional failure. The wealthy are building private, parallel systems while public services wither. Most responses to this come in two flavors: denial, or individual retreat into prepping and personal escape.

Place-based organizing offers a third path. Neither utopian vision nor dystopian withdrawal — just the practical work of rebuilding from the ground up. Rooted in the particular places where we actually live, and connected across regions to other communities doing similar work.
