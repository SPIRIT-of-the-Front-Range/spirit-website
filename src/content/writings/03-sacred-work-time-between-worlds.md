We are living through something unprecedented — a moment when things are coming apart faster than we can make sense of them. This isn't just happening "out there" in our systems and structures; it's happening inside each of us. **We are the threshold between worlds.**

This talk explores what it means to use the tools of the institutions we have to grow something new — and how everyday, place-based practice (not just annual gatherings) becomes the foundation for the kind of civilization our communities are trying to build.
