*Solidarity Suppers are how we practice the Commons before we formalize them.*

We share a long table. We listen. We host Listeners' Councils, storytellings, and the kind of slow conversation that weaves deeper sensings of this time we are in — and how we can care for each other through it.

These gatherings center community leaders and elders, particularly our Indigenous relatives — the ones whose knowledge of this place precedes any of our institutions.
