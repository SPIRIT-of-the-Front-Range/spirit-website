*"What is it to be in the context of the Commons?"*

Monthly open sessions, no prerequisites. We trace the arc from when all peoples were indigenous to a place, through the enclosures that privatized shared land, to the present movement of re-commoning.

Each Teach-In produces resource lists and course materials, building toward a publicly available curriculum and ebook on the Commons.
