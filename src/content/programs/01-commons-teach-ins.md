*"What does it mean to be in and of the Commons?"*

Sporadic monthly sessions in which we trace the arc from all peoples being indigenous to their places, through the enclosures that privatized shared land, to the present movement of re-commoning.

Each Teach-In produces resource lists and course materials, building toward a publicly available curriculum and ebook on the Commons.
