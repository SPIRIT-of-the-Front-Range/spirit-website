A free 12-session curriculum, in partnership with the Neighborhood Villaging Project, that takes a small group from *awareness* to *action.*

The arc:

1. Building the Foundation
2. Getting to Know Your Neighbors
3. Risk Assessment & Site Visits
4. Essential Skills — water, power, first aid
5. Disaster Recovery & a Scenario Drill
6. Capstone — your neighborhood's Community Resiliency Plan

Potlucks and long walks are part of the curriculum. Solidarity is the foundation; preparedness is the consequence.
