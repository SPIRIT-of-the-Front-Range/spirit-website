We are fundraising in order to allocate resources directly to regenerative ecological and cultural projects along the Front Range — according to the enfranchised will of the denizens of the Commons.

Allocation Rounds use quadratic funding to surface what the community most cares about, with no individual or corporation enclosing the process.

The first round is being designed now. Subscribe at *Connect* to be notified when applications open.
